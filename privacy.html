GitPrice

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 11, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 11, 2026

GitPrice ("we", "us", or "our") is a browser extension that helps you compare prices across Egyptian e-commerce stores including Amazon.eg, Noon, Jumia, B.Tech, and 2B. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

1. Information We Collect

GitPrice collects the minimum data necessary to provide price comparison functionality.

Data collected automatically while using the extension:

  • Product identifiers (ASIN, SKU) and product titles from the pages you browse on supported stores
  • Current prices displayed on those pages
  • Your extension settings (e.g. background search frequency, notifications preference) stored in chrome.storage.sync
  • Anonymous error and crash reports (opt-out available in Settings → Privacy)

Account data (if you sign in):

  • Email address used to create your GitPrice account
  • Authentication tokens stored locally in chrome.storage.local

We do not collect your browsing history outside supported store pages, financial information, passwords, or any personally identifiable information beyond your email if you register.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To search for and display competing prices across supported stores
  • To send price-drop notifications for products you are tracking (if enabled)
  • To improve extension reliability by diagnosing crashes and errors
  • To authenticate your account and manage Pro subscription status

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services and Affiliate Links

GitPrice may redirect you to Amazon.eg, Noon, Jumia, B.Tech, or 2B using affiliate links. These links allow us to earn a small commission when you make a purchase, at no additional cost to you. Affiliate links are provided for your convenience and do not affect the prices you see or pay.

When you follow an affiliate link, the destination store's own privacy policy applies to your activity on their site. GitPrice does not share your personal information with affiliate stores.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your extension settings and authentication tokens are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage APIs (chrome.storage.local and chrome.storage.sync). Server-side data (e.g. your account and price history) is stored on secured servers with access controls and encryption at rest.

We retain price comparison data only as long as necessary to provide the service. Account data is retained until you delete your account.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Third-party vendors who assist us in operating the extension (e.g. hosting, error tracking) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements: If required by applicable law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to users

6. Your Rights (GDPR and General)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Opt-out of error reporting: Disable anonymous error reports in Settings → Privacy

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@gitprice.com.

7. Cookies and Tracking

The GitPrice extension itself does not set cookies. When you visit store pages, those sites may set their own cookies — their respective privacy policies govern that activity.

8. Children's Privacy

GitPrice is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy on this page with an updated effective date. For significant changes, we will notify you via a notice in the extension.
